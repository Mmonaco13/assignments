My Education

Hampton Street Elementary School

Social Studies

One day, some of the other kids were throwing rocks at me. My friend Jenny told me to run so I did. Somehow, while I was running, my leg braces came off, and I was able to run like the wind. I never had to wear my leg braces after that.

Alma Bryant High School

US History

One day after school, some boys were chasing me for some reason. I accidentally ran into a football practice. This college coach saw how fast I ran and wanted me to play for his football team at the University of Alabama.

University of Alabama

HIS 100 Early European History

I played for the schools football team for 5 years. I must have been really good, because they told me I was on a new team called the All-American team. My new team all went to the White House and I got to meet President Kennedy.
