My Time in Vietnam

Signing up for the US Army

After I graduated from College, I didn't really have a plan. A man convinced me to enlist for the United States Army. It was on the way to camp that I met my friend Bubba. Bubba loved talking about shrimp. He told me about all the different ways you can cook shrimp. He told me how he plans on buying a shrimp boat and I thought it was such a great idea that I wanted to join him.

Basic Training

Training was not too hard. We had to scrub the floor with toothbrushes, but it wasnt too bad. One of the drill sergants was very impressed with how fast I assembled my rifle. I was able to go back home after training. I told my friend Jenny that I was being deployed to Vietnam. She didnt seem to happy, and told me to not be brave.

Vietnam

In Vietnam, Bubba and I were in the same infantry division. This is where I met my friend Lieutenant Dan. One day, we got ambushed. I was told to retreat but I got lost. When I couldn't find Bubba, I got scared that he was hurt. I saved a few other soldiers but I was too late to help Bubba. After the ambush, I realized that I was shot in the buttocks and eventually got sent home.
